A neurotwin couples a subject-specific head model with whole-brain network dynamics, so that stimulation protocols can be designed *in silico* before they reach the patient. The approach targets conditions such as epilepsy and Alzheimer's disease, turning brain stimulation from one-size-fits-all into an individually optimized therapy.
