The Galvani project is a flagship ERC Synergy grant uniting expertise across computational modeling, in-vitro and in-vivo electrophysiology, and clinical neurology. The goal is to understand precisely how weak electrical currents influence neural circuits — and to leverage that understanding for treatment of drug-resistant epilepsy. Results feed directly into clinical trial design at partner hospitals across Europe.
