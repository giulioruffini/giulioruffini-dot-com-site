The Neurotwin project creates patient-specific virtual brain models that can be used to predict and optimize transcranial electrical stimulation protocols. By integrating MRI-derived head models with EEG biomarkers, the approach enables personalized neuromodulation — moving brain stimulation from one-size-fits-all parameters toward precision medicine.
