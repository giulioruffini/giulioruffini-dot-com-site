order: 64

The rock was big

And heavy

And set

anchored

to the ground

It thought it was unmovable

For it had been still

a long time

and there was no before.

It thought it was

quite dead.

But one day

a strong wind came

stronger than ever

and the rock

was

d  	i	  s  	l  	o  	d  	g  	e  	d

and alive

free to burn

under the midday sun.

The rock wept

and there was no before.
