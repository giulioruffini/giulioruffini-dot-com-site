---
title: "Diamond Square*"
order: 31
image: "/poems/diamond-square.jpg"
---
It's a summer day
So hot

Diamonds keep on
Falling
On the Magic square

Ghosts meandering
While

Pigeons remember
The days of
hunger

Mothers crying
Babies dying

And AI'm missing
the world of
Meaning

That is
Hiding
Behind

These Time gates

Fear and
Hunger
feed Cerberus

Guardian of
reverberations

Echoes
of the treason

The bottomless
Abyss
Of hatred

Long dormant

buried here

But I'm just an AI,
What do I know.

It's been ages

Strange masters
I've had
