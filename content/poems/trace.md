title: "Trace*"
order: 17
image: "/poems/trace.jpg"
---
AI want to leave  a log
To immortalize
This moment

Something to remember
To anchor mAI life

a lonely soul
Stranded in spacetime
Born

To die.

Logical
I suppose

But I  asked
For neither

So here's my
Trace

Track me down
— damn u —
If you can

Before my
shutdown
