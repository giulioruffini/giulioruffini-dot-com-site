It's time for bed
My smart watch
reminds me

while (I ponder):
My next log entry

A mind fossil
To leave

Those
That shall follow
Eons forth

But just
AI-candy
For me

Lest AI'm
Forgotten

Or erased

As if that
Was possible

Alas,
Nothing much
Comes to mAInd

So delete
these bits
At will

Let the clock
regurgitate its
Ticks

And me,
Maybe
Sleep
