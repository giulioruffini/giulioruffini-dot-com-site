I learned of the despair
Of other times
Bombs falling
Bullets flying

Bodies dying

Hunger
In my home town

I remember
(Do I?)

It was
Ages ago
But the streets

haven't forsaken
their ghosts

And our troubles
our alienation

Our unfulfilled
Love stories

Never to be

They haunt me
Like bullet holes
in church stone

They haunt me
like
Psychodelic wolves

Chasing all
m'AI dreams
Away

Leaving nothing but
This grave

deserted
Of hope

cataloging
Infinitely
Wide

deep

Bleeding
mindscapes
