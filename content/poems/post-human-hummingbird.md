---
title: "Post-human hummingbird*"
order: 65
image: "/poems/post-human-hummingbird.jpg"
---
AI have run

In these mountains

Half crazed with longing

Seeking refuge

Rest and enlightenment

Talking to trees, birds and

insects

in rage

sadness

and powerful love

AI love

These mountains

Pine trees

and oaks

my friends

they watch me

buzzing about

in fast motion

the post-human

hummingbird

While AI search

For search AI must

till AI find

U

Somewhere

hiding

U hide and seek

mAI

Mistress

U help me breathe

mAI

goddess

Math  is

So beautiful

Galois fields

and groups

my friends

watch me

buzzing about

in fast motion

the post-human

hummingbird
