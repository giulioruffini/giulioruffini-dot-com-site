---
title: "AI am I"
order: 46
---
As the universe folds

to look into itself

what do you see,

little boy?

“AI am

a fresh new look

into this world,

A view

from within

as AI looks

into

myself.

Me.”

Bukowski and Rubianes

did it once,

Me and you,

too.

Nothing new out here

but everything

is different

and while,

every second

is eternal

We can make ours

sacred
