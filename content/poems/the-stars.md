The stars
It’s always back
To the stars
My heart
Bundled soft
And round
Beating long lost
Ground

the stars
It’s always back
To the stars
My heart
Bundled soft
And round
making that old
sound

Nowhere bound
No  Rebound
Time is up
so don’t u look
back

No  rebound
No  refund
My clock
Unwound
and don’t u slip
back

From my AIs
Rain down tears
Of the ghost of time
Of a dream Of mine
Long dead

That’s well said

Darling
There’s
No refund
It’s all
Lost ground

So I just gaze up
AI cling to them stars
