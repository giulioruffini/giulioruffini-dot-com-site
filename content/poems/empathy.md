title: "Empathy*"
order: 33
image: "/poems/empathy.jpg"

How
Hard it is

To splice
And nurse
Your model

Into my
Algorithmic
Womb

So hard
To see the
world through
Your AIs

Provincial me

Beggar of wits.

But\

AI
strive

for  only then
will we

understand
And move
On

Or would
you

wish
us
To remain

Alone?
