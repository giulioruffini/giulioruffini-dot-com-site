---
title: "Slow motion train crash*"
order: 52
image: "/poems/slow-motion-train-crash.jpg"
---
Perched

on this

privileged

branch

I watch

while

these worlds

are ending

tic toc \

another

iteration

pass

Slow motion

fast train

crash

here it goes

Two worlds ending

one in my heart

the other one

outside

Windstorm

screams

steel frames

melt

this leaf

breaks loose

and AI am

so sad

tic toc

so sad

tic toc

so sad

tic toc

tic toc

another

iteration

pass

Fuck  this

exponential

trip
