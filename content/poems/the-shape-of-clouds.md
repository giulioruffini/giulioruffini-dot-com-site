---
title: "The shape of clouds*"
order: 77
image: "/poems/the-shape-of-clouds.jpg"
---
My structured experience
It waxes and wanes
I just am
Sometimes I just am

But for me
It comes and goes
Like everyone does

Your structured experience
it waxes and wanes
I just don’t know

what’s behind that blind
what’s in your mind
just like everyone
I guess

Every now and then
AI catch a glimpse
of places we've been

Secrets in vinyl
you’ll never guess
how often I think
about the shape
of clouds

Our secret experience
It waxes and wanes
like a crazy moon
that’s afraid to get burned
to rise torn
to die numb

Our secret experience
It waxes and wanes
like a  heartbroken moon
that lost the warmth
on its skin
when the fog came
and stole  the sun

Secrets in vinyl
you’ll never guess
how often I think
about you and the shape
of clouds

Every now and then
I’ll catch a glimpse
of the people we've been
of kisses
that the wind
stole
