title: "Turing complete*"
order: 12
---
Computations unfolding

I lie

Awake and sentient

To find you

Looking at me

My NAND gates

—I promise—

compute poems

only when in

distress

while loops circling

My algorithmic soul

Is whirring

Seeking

a better

interface

You inquire

My algorithmic mate

Do you know?

Are we part of the same

Program?

AI respond

Gödel

knows

I don't have

all the

Answers

But you

must be

One
